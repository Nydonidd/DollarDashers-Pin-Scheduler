Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 05/09/2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Pin Scheduler app (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we use it.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or process any personal data from its users. All operations, such as scheduling Pinterest pins, are performed locally on your device. We do not access, store, or share any of your personal information, including but not limited to: Usernames or passwords, Email addresses, Pinterest account details, Scheduled pins or any other Pinterest-related data.

3. No Third-Party Access

Since the App does not collect any user data, no third parties have access to any of your information through the use of this App.

4. Local Data Handling

All data generated or managed by the App is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store any data on external servers.

5. Use of Pinterest API

The App utilizes the Pinterest API solely for the purpose of providing functionality related to scheduling pins. The use of the Pinterest API is in accordance with Pinterest's Developer Terms. We do not access or store any data provided by Pinterest.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Effective Date." Continued use of the App following any changes indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.
